Dynamic Dashboard

Junaid Khan March 11, 2026

We introduced a new Dynamic Dashboard in Fillvisa Plus to give legal professionals and their clients a clearer overview of their work. The dashboard surfaces key information such as recent cases, forms in progress, and quick actions, allowing users to navigate their active workload without having to manually browse through different sections of the application.

 

 

 

The dashboard updates automatically as cases and forms change, helping users quickly see what requires attention. This release focuses on improving day-to-day workflow by making important information easier to access immediately after login, reducing the need to search through multiple pages to find active work.
